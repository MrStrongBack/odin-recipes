Mexicana Tinga

Description

Tinga is a Mexican dish made with shredded chicken in a sauce made from tomatoes, chipotle chilis in adobo, and sliced onions.
It is often served on a tostada and accompanied by a layer of refried beans.

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 pieces of chicken breast
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • 1 7oz can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 31 oz can refried beans
  • 4 sun tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon garlic power
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • Shredded Mozzarella(optional)
  • lettuce(optional)

Steps

  1. Grab a medium sized pot, fill the pot up half way with water. Bring the water to a boil and add the chicken breast to cook.
    Cook the chicken for around 20 to 25 minutes.
  2. Once the chicken is cooked, grab a hand mixer or a fork and slowly shred the chicken. Once the chicken is shredded put it off
    to the side for later.
  3. In another pot put in the tomatoes and cover with water, bring to a simmer over medium heat. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes. While that
    is cooking slice and prep the onions.
  4. Place the tomato mixture, garlic, salt, pepper, cumin, cayenne pepper, paparika and the chipotle can into a blender.
    Once blended put the chicken and the blended mixture into a sauce pan on simmer. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes and stir occasionally.
  5. Now for the final step in the process is cooking your can of refried beans in a small sauce pan on medium heat, add some mozzarella for flavor

    6. For Serving

    • 1 bag of tostadas
    • queso fresco
    • crumbling cheese
    • Honduran Cream

    Serving Steps

    1. Place a toastada on a plate, then put some refried beans on top.
    2. Next you will add the chicken on the refried beans, as much as you'd like.
    3. Now add the Honduran cream next, and we will finish it off with the crumbling cheese on top.
      Now your Tinga is hot and ready to eat!
