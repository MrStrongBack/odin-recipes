Fantastic Teramisu

Description

A creamy dessert of espresso-soaked ladyfingers surrounded by lightly sweetened whipped cream and a rich mascarpone,
tiramisu relies heavily on the quality of its ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 large egg yolks
  • half a cup of grandulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup or 8 ounces of mascarpone
  • 1 3/4 cups columbian coffee
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 1 pack of ladyfingers
  • 1 to 2 ounces of chocolate shavings(optional)

Steps

  1. Using an electric mixer in a medium bowl, whip together egg yolks and ¼ cup/50 grams
    sugar until very pale yellow and about tripled in volume. A slight ribbon should fall
    from the beaters (or whisk attachment) when lifted from the bowl. Transfer mixture to a
    large bowl, wiping out the medium bowl used to whip the yolks and set aside.
  2. In the medium bowl, whip cream and remaining ¼ cup/50 grams sugar until it creates soft-medium peaks.
    Add mascarpone and continue to whip until it creates a soft, spreadable mixture with medium peaks.
    Gently fold the mascarpone mixture into the sweetened egg yolks until combined.
  3. Combine espresso and rum in a shallow bowl and set aside.
  4. Quickly dip each lady finger into the espresso mixure, be wary of oversoaking each one.
    Place them round side up until each section is complete, then pour your mixture on top of
    each layer until your dish is filled, or you run out of mixture.
  5. Dust the top layer with your cocoa shavings to top off this amazing dessert.
  6. Enjoy this teramisu!
