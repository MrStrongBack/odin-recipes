Tacos Al Pastor

Description:

Al Pastor is a traditional Mexican dish made from various spicy seasoning on marinated pork. It usually begins with your choice of tortilla stacked with
thin shavings of marinated, roasted pork, with pineapple, pico de gallo, and hints of lime juice accompined with cilantro.
